# Value Proposition

## Overview
To address the course selection challenges faced by international students and first-year undergraduate students, this algorithm-driven course selection software provides a centralized and structured platform that improves efficiency, accuracy, and confidence in course decision-making.
The platform responds to validated user needs from interviews and tests, including fragmented course information across multiple systems, unclear course prerequisites, difficulty comparing electives, system navigation challenges (especially for international students), and uncertainty in course-to-career alignment.
It centralizes all course-related information (including course descriptions, prerequisites, timetables, and degree requirements) into a single platform, reducing the need to switch between multiple systems. It also provides intelligent filtering of elective courses based on degree, major, credit requirements, and eligibility, helping students identify suitable options more efficiently.
For international students, the system includes simplified onboarding and guided navigation to reduce unfamiliarity with the university registration system.

# Core Value Delivered
Centralized course information system
All course-related information is integrated into one platform, reducing fragmentation and search effort across multiple systems.

Intelligent elective filtering and comparison
Courses are filtered based on degree, major, credit requirements, and eligibility, allowing students to quickly identify and compare suitable electives.

Clear prerequisite visualization
Course prerequisites and dependencies are clearly displayed to help students avoid ineligible selections and understand course progression.

Decision support for academic and career alignment
The platform helps students understand how course choices relate to degree progression and potential career pathways.

International student support features
Step-by-step onboarding, simplified terminology, and visual study planning tools reduce system navigation confusion.

# Key Benefits
Reduces time spent searching across multiple platforms
Improves efficiency in course comparison and selection
Minimises errors in course enrolment decisions
Increases clarity in academic planning
Improves confidence in decision-making
Supports international students with system navigation
