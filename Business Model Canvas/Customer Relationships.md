The customer relationships are based on key insights from interviews
### Self service:
Students prefer independent and efficient tools.
- Students can use the platform without external support
- Instant access to course eligibility, prerequisites, and planning tools
- Automated warnings and recommendations reduce reliance on others
### Guided Support
Students need support during decision-making, not just information access.
- Step-by-step onboarding for first-time users
- Visual guidance for course pathways and prerequisites
- Clear summaries instead of complex descriptions
### Peer Informed Support
Students trust peer experiences more than official sources.
- Course reviews and feedback from other students
- Social proof increases confidence in decisions
### Trust & Transparency Mechanism
- Clear indication of data origin
- Regular updates to maintain reliability
