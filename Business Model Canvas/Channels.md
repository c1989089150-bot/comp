# Channels
Our solution will be delivered through the following mediums:
1) A Website: This will act as our main delivery channel. Students will be able to access the website to check prerequisites and learn what courses they need to enrol in. 
2) Search engines: Students will be able to find our website through various search engines when searching for information regarding course enrolment. This will redirect them to our website, enablin them to use and access our tool
3) University Referrals: Students will be able to find our solution through links found throughout various student portals, especially when inquiring about enrolment. This ensures that students who are looking for help, will be able to easily access and use our tool to assist them in enrolment
4) Social Media and Student Communities: This solution could be shared through various social media pages (e.g. student facebook groups, group chats, etc.). Many students tend to rely on advice from their peers when seeking help, and so this tool could be shared in these communities as a way to get help in course enrolment. 
These channels aim to ensure our solution is easy to find, even for international students and first years.
