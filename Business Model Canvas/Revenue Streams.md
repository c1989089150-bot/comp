# Revenue Streams

Our solution will be able to generate revenue based on the following streams:
1) University partnerships
Since our business model aims to make the enrolment process easier, the platform could also collaborate with 
approved university services or student support programs.
2) Tiered student service
The platfrom will provide essential course planning functions for all students, such as semester available courses 
and eligible courses. And also, the platform will offer a more advanced level of service. This service will contain
more personalised support, such as risk alerts,tailored course recommendations and further guidance 
based on a student’s degree progress and future goals.
