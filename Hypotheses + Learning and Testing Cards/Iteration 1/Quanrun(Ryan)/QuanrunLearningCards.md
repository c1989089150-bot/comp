# Learn Card 1
## Hypothesis: 
We believe the lack of filtered elective options causes students to waste time and make incorrect course choices.
## Observation: 
Students reported spending hours browsing irrelevant electives and worrying about selecting courses that do not meet their degree or major requirements (Interviews 1 & 3).
## Conclusion: 
Students need a filtered list of electives tailored to their specific degree, major, and remaining course requirements.
## Next Step: 
Develop a filter function in the course selection tool that restricts elective options to only those eligible for the student’s program and progress.

# Learn Card 2
## Hypothesis: 
We believe manual course enrollment increases the risk of missing core courses or enrolling in conflicting classes.
## Observation: 
Several students shared experiences of accidentally missing core course enrollment deadlines or selecting courses with overlapping class times (Interviews 2 & 4).
## Conclusion:
Students would benefit from automatic enrollment for core courses and conflict checks for all selected classes.
## Next Step: 
Integrate an automatic core course enrollment feature and a class time conflict checker into the course selection tool.

# Learn Card 3
## Hypothesis: 
We believe international students face additional confusion due to unfamiliarity with the university’s course registration system.
## Observation: 
International students mentioned struggling with the university’s existing registration platform, including unclear navigation and language barriers (Interviews 5 & 6).
## Conclusion: 
International students need a user-friendly, simplified course selection tool with clear instructions and multilingual support.
## Next Step: 
Design a simplified interface for the course selection tool and add multilingual (English/Chinese) guidance to reduce navigation and language barriers.
