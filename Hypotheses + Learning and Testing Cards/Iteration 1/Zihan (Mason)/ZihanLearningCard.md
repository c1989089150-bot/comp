# Learn Card 1
## Hypothesis: 
We believe that a more detailed display demonstrating information in the assessment part is very important to students
## Observation: 
We have observed that students generally consider the assessment section of a course to be crucial when selecting courses. However, current assessment information is often presented too briefly, making it difficult for students to intuitively understand the requirements and difficulty of the assessments.（interview 1，2，3）
## Conclusion: 
From that, we learn that students need to have a clearer understanding of the details of the assessment, such as the assessment method, whether there is a review, and what the task actually entails.
## Next Step: 
Therefore, we will design a course selection tool that will present more detailed information about the assessment section, allowing students to more intuitively understand the difficulty of the tasks and helping them choose appropriate courses.
