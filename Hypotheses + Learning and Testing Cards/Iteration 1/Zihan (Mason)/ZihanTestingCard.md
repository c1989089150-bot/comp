# Test Card
## Hypothesis
We believe that if the platform could display more detailed information about the assessment components, allowing students to intuitively understand the difficulty level and assessment methods, course selection would be much easier for them.

## Test
To verify that, we will display more information about the assessment and difficulty ratings on the platform.

## Metric
We will measure whether this information is very helpful for their course selection.

## Criteria
If most students say that this information about assessment requirements and difficulty levels helps them choose courses, then our hypothesis is correct.
