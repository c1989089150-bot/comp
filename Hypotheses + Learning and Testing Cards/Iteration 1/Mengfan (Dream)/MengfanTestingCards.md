## Test Card 1
# Hypothesis: 
We believe that students struggle to plan their degree because course order and prerequisites are unclear.
# Test: 
To verify that, we will ask students about difficulties in identifying course order and prerequisites when selecting courses.
# Metric: 
And measure how often students report confusion about course sequences and prerequisites.
# Criteria: 
We are right if multiple students indicate confusion or errors in course planning due to unclear prerequisites.
# Interview Question: 
Therefore, we will ask “What information do you wish had been clearer or easier to find when you were choosing your courses?”
