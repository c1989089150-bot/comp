# LEARNING CARD 1
## Hypothesis:
We believed that all students encountered difficulties when enrolling in courses, due to the disorganised method of enrolment. 

## Observations
We observed that not all students had difficulty enrolling. Most difficulties were encountered by the international students, who found the process more difficult due to language barriers, unfamiliar platforms.

## Conclusion
From that we learned that the enrolment process does not affect all students equally. International students therefore need more support in the enrolment process to ensure it is more accomodating. 

## Next Step
Therefore, we will now focus the apps features on assisting international students in enrolling. This issue is particularly important for intnerational students, as they also often pay higher fees for courses, and so misenrolment can cause significant financial detriment. 


# LEARNING CARD 2
## Hypothesis:
We believed that the primary issue encountered by students regarding course enrolment was difficulties finding out what courses to enrol in. 

## Observations
We obsereved during the interview process, that a major issue students encountered was actually enrolling in courses they felt were irrelevent to their degree or desired career path. 

## Conclusion
We learned that there is a secondary issue focused not on just the actual process of enrolling, but also enrolling in relevant and useful courses. Students felt poorly informed regarding the actual content they were going to be learning in their courses. 

## Next Step
Therefore, we will perform more research into this particular issue, and see if there is a simple solution we may be able to develop or integrate into our application.
