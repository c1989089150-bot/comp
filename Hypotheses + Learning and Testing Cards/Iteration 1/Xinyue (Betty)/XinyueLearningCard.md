# Learning Card
## Hypothesis
We believed that the main issue for course selection was students do not knowing which courses they needed to choose.

## Observation
We noticed in the interviews that students were not only unsure about which courses to choose, 
but also concerned about missing prerequisites or taking a course at the wrong time.

## Conclusion
We learned that the issue is not only about identifying the correct courses, 
but also about understanding the correct order and timing of enrolment.

## Next Step
Therefore, we will focus on warn student about prerequisite issues, course sequencing problems,
and courses that are only available in certain semesters.
