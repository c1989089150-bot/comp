# Test Card
## Hypothesis
We believe that students will find course selection easier if the platform
can warn them about prerequisites, course order, and semester availability.

## Test
To verify that, we will show students a prototype of the platform that includes 
alerts for missing prerequisites, incorrect course order, and courses that are 
only offered in certain semesters.

## Metric
We will measure whether students feel the platform makes course selection easier.

## Criteria
We are right if most students say that these warnings make the enrolment process clearer 
and help them avoid mistakes.
